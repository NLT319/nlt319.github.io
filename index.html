Mechanical engineer with an interest in mechanical designs, embedded controls, robotics, and research. I recently completed my BS/MS at UC Santa Barbara, where I did jumping robot research in the Hawkes Lab.

Selected work

Cooper, a jumping robot that reorients itself midair with a reaction wheel so it can recoup its jump energy through the leg.

A running shoe sole that reached 87.4% energy return, beating the Nike Alphafly benchmark in our testing.

MEMS sidewall characterization that traced a KOH etch angle sitting well below its theoretical 54.7° down to a crystal-plane misalignment, using profilometry, FIB-milled SEM, and XRD.
