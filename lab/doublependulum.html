Double pendulum

Grab either mass and throw it. The trace follows the second mass, and since the system is chaotic, two throws that look identical will diverge within a few swings.

energy: 0 J · throw me

How it works

The full nonlinear equations of motion are integrated with RK4 at 240 steps per second, and the energy readout is really a check on that integrator: it should hold nearly constant with damping off and decay smoothly with it on. Flinging works by tracking the pointer's recent angular motion and handing the released mass that velocity.
