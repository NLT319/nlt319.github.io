Lab

Small interactive experiments live here, mostly physics toys built to see what runs well on a static site. Nothing on this page is finished, and some of it never will be.

  • Cart-pole, where you balance an inverted pendulum by hand, or let an LQR do it and try to knock it over.
  • Stick the landing, a cartoon of Cooper launched on an arc with a random tumble that you have to set down foot first.
  • Suspension tuner, a car driving a randomly generated road with a PID controller at each wheel that you can tune as it drives.
  • Double pendulum, a sandbox you can grab and throw, with an energy readout to watch the damping happen.
