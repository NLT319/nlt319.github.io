Cart-pole

Keep the pendulum upright. Arrow keys or the buttons push the cart, and once you have survived a few seconds, random shoves start arriving, each telegraphed by an arrow a moment before it hits. The LQR button toggles control over to a controller, and then you can try to knock it over.

upright: 0.0 s · best: 0.0 s · u = 0 N

How it works

The nonlinear cart-pole dynamics are integrated with RK4 at 240 steps per second. The LQR gain matrix is computed in your browser at load time by iterating the discrete Riccati equation on a numerically linearized model of the same dynamics, and in LQR mode your pushes and the scheduled shoves enter the loop as disturbance forces the controller has to reject.
